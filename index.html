It worked right away with ChatGPT

Why do we so often choose the easy way? “It worked right away with ChatGPT” is a design toolkit and experiment that uses AI and vibe coding to expand creative possibilities—without any proprietary, closed models. Developed by students at Darmstadt University of Applied Sciences, it proves that open language models can empower designers and enable novel approaches. A call to use AI not just for convenience, but consciously.
